Signature Karcher 2-Step Exterior Wash

$40

Restore your vehicle's shine right at your doorstep. Our core service is designed to provide a safer, more effective clean than a traditional car wash, minimizing the risk of scratches while maximizing brilliance.

Perfect for: The vehicle owner who wants a professional, scratch-free exterior clean that keeps their car's paint protected and looking its best.

What's Included:
  • Step 1: Karcher Pre-Wash Foam Bath to lift heavy dirt and grime without contact.
  • Step 2: Gentle Two-Bucket Contact Wash using soft microfiber mitts to prevent swirl marks.
  • Wheels, Tires & Wheel Wells are thoroughly cleaned to dissolve brake dust.
  • Gentle Hand Dry with plush microfiber towels to prevent water spots.
  • Tires Dressed for a clean, satin finish.
